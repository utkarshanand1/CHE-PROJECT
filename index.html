Applications Creating Environmental Awarness

Ecosia

Ecosia is a web browser application that uses its ad revenue to plant trees. Users can search the web using Ecosia, and a portion of the advertising revenue generated from their searches is used to fund tree planting projects worldwide. Ecosia provides updates on the number of trees planted, helping users track their environmental impact and learn about reforestation efforts.

read more>>

TrashOut

TrashOut is a mobile application that enables users to report and map litter and illegal dumpsites. Users can take photos of littered areas and report them through the app, which then generates a map of reported trash sites. This data can be used by local authorities and environmental organizations to identify litter hotspots and take action to clean up the environment.

read more>>

iNaturalist

iNaturalist is a citizen science application that allows users to identify and report observations of plants and animals in nature. Users can upload photos and descriptions of species they encounter, and the app uses machine learning algorithms to help identify the species. The data collected through iNaturalist contributes to scientific research and helps raise awareness about biodiversity and conservation.

read more>>

Good On You

Good On You is a mobile application that provides information about the sustainability and ethical practices of fashion brands. Users can search for brands and get ratings based on their environmental impact, labor conditions, and animal welfare. Good On You also offers recommendations for sustainable alternatives, empowering users to make informed choices and support brands that align with their values.

read more>>

JouleBug

JouleBug is a mobile application that encourages users to adopt sustainable habits in their daily lives through gamification. Users can earn points and badges for completing green actions such as conserving energy, reducing waste, and using public transportation. JouleBug also provides educational tips and challenges to help users learn about environmental issues and take action to reduce their impact.
